Our History

The University of Agriculture Faisalabad, established in 1906, is one of the premier agricultural universities in Pakistan. It has played a fundamental role in agricultural education, research, and extension since its inception.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strive for excellence in education, research, and outreach programs to empower farmers, students, and researchers alike, contributing to sustainable agricultural development.

Campus & Facilities

The campus is home to modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, research farms, and extensive libraries providing a rich learning environment. Students benefit from a vibrant academic community fostering innovation and practical knowledge.
